myScheme is a National Platform that aims to offer one-stop search and discovery of the Government schemes. It provides an innovative, technology-based solution to discover scheme information based upon the eligibility of the citizen.

myScheme helps citizens find the right Government schemes for them without visiting multiple government websites. It provides personalized recommendations based on eligibility criteria and guides users on how to apply for different schemes.

myScheme helps you discover and learn about government schemes. For actual applications, you will be directed to the official government portals or websites of the respective departments where you can complete your application process.

Simply enter your personal details like age, gender, location, income, occupation, etc. Our system will analyze your information and show you all the government schemes you're eligible for, along with detailed information about each scheme.

You can find comprehensive information including scheme objectives, eligibility criteria, benefits offered, application process, required documents, important dates, and links to official application portals.
